Benefits of Kung Fu

  • strengthens external and internal muscles, including core muscles
  • tones up involuntary muscles of the internal organs
  • improves blood and chi氣 circulation throughout all the meridians
  • cultivates chi氣 (energy) and jing精 (vitality) in the dan tien丹田(reservoir below the belly button)
  • builds anaerobic and aerobic conditionings
  • calms the mind and spirit神
  • toughens bone, skin, muscles, sinews, and mind
  • enhances function and health of the eleven internal organs五臟六腑
  • speed up reflexes and agility
  • harmonizes the yin陰 and yang陽 in the body
  • learns self-defense techniques
  • increases flexibility of muscles and joints
  • achieves better stability and balance
  • relieves stress and tension physically and mentally
  • burns calories and lose weight while having fun
  • develops an outstanding character by understanding our values and philosophy
  • understands the camaraderie of a kung fu family
  • keeps ones looking young and radiant
  • prolongs quality of life and longevity

Values

  • Loyalty 忠誠 – commit to your art, school, marriage, friends, work
  • Respect 尊敬 – respect your teacher, sifu, parent, elderly, ancestor
  • Honor 尊嚴 – have integrity and dignity to yourself, family, and the art of kung fu
  • Kindness 仁慈 – be kind and compassionate to all living things
  • Courage 勇敢 – conquer your fear
  • Righteousness 義 – enforce justice by defending the weak from unfairness
  • Moral 道德 – know right from wrong behavior
  • Strength 強壯 – keep your body strong including muscles, chi, bone, sinew
  • Humility 謙虛 – be humble and modest
